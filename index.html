Why Us

Our goal at MEIKLES FOUNDATION is to create a sustainable ecosystem where the vulnerable and disadvantaged in Zimbabwe are empowered

To care for and protect those who aree most in need ,often children ,the youths and the elderly ,with a special focus on providing sponsorship and learning for all youth and women in Zimbabwe .To promote sustainable development through initiatives protecting wildlife and the environment to create an

interactive learning and proffessional environment where local businesses can come together .To protect local artist and contribute to artistic excellence by funding initiatives that promote achievement in the literary ,visual and performing arts ,and to encourage the people of Zimbabwe to appreciate such excellence
